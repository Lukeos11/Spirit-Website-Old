Some information about Spirit


Spirit is a fully custom Discord bot made to make your server light up and be amazing using just the one bot.

By inviting Spirit you will get access to most commands that will allow you to moderate, manage and complete your Discord server.

Moderation (WIP)

When you invite Spirit you get access to all the commands you need but one important aspect is moderation.

The moderation commands have some amazing abilities that will help you keep out the bad people and keep the good ones in.

Suggestions

Suggestions are an amazing way to help Spirit out!

Just use the suggestion command or post it in the suggestions channel in the Discord server to make a suggestion.
